Prof. Dora Akunyili

The Brave woman that spearheaded the eradication of counterfeit drugs & unsafe food in Nigeria

Here's a time line of Prof. Dora Akunyili's Life:

  • 1954 - Born on 14th July
  • 1966 - Attended St Patrick's Primary School for Elementary School
  • 1973 - Proceeded to Queen of the Rosary Secondary School and finished with honors which earned her the Eastern Nigerian Government Post primary scholarship & Federal Government Undergraduate Scholarship.
  • 1978 - Obtained first degree in Pharmacology
  • 1981 - Became a Graduate Assistant in Fau.NN of Pharmaceutical Sciences UNN.
  • 1985 - Obtained Phd from University of Nigeria, Nsukka.
  • 1990 - She became a Senior Lecturer.
  • 1996 - She was made a Consultant Pharmacologist at the College of Medicine
  • 1996 - She became the Zonal Secretary of the Petroleum Special Trust Fund (PTF) cordinating projects funded by profits from oil in Nigeria's south eastern states.
  • 2001 - Appointed Director General of National Agency for Food and Drug Administration and control (NAFDAC) and served till 2009
  • 2009 - Appointed Minister of Information and Communication.
  • 2010 - Resigned position as Minister of Information and Communication to run for Senatorial seat.
  • 2014 - Died of Cancer in India.

"Prof Dora Akunyili is one of the best that came out of Nigeria. Her passion to change the old order saw her save many lives in her capacity as the NAFDAC DG . We must continue to celebrate her efforts and achievements so that this generation and the one yet to come will see reason to work hard for the Nation and humanity."

-- Ifeanyi Ubah
